Dyson V8 Blue Light Fix

If your Dyson V8 shows a constant blue light but won’t run, try this:

  1. Fully charge the battery — plug it in for at least 3.5 hours.
  2. Press and hold the trigger for 20 seconds while the vacuum is off the charger.
  3. Remove the filter, give it a full wash under cold water, and let it dry for 24 hours.
  4. Check for clogs in the wand or bin. Clean them thoroughly.
  5. Reassemble and try turning it on again.

If the issue persists, the battery may need replacement.
