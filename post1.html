Samsung TV Firmware 2025

Having trouble updating your Samsung TV firmware in 2025? Here’s a quick fix that works for most models:

Steps:

  • Grab a USB stick and format it to FAT32.
  • Visit the Samsung Support site on your computer.
  • Search your exact TV model (e.g., UA55TU8000WXXY) and download the latest firmware file.
  • Unzip the downloaded file, then move the folder (not just the files inside) onto the USB stick.
  • Insert USB into your TV and go to: Settings > Support > Software Update > Update via USB.

If the TV says “no update file found,” make sure the folder structure is correct and you didn’t accidentally move just the contents.
