LG Fridge Leaking Water

Noticed a puddle under your LG fridge? Here’s how to fix the leak fast:

  1. Unplug the fridge and move it away from the wall.
  2. Check the drain pan underneath for cracks or overflow — empty it if full.
  3. Look for blockages in the defrost drain at the back — use warm water or a pipe cleaner to clear it.
  4. Inspect the water line if your fridge has a dispenser or ice maker — tighten or replace if leaking.
  5. Wipe down everything and monitor for future leaks.

Most leaks come from simple blockages — this DIY can save you hundreds.
