Samsung Washer Not Draining

If your Samsung washing machine won’t drain, try this quick reset:

  1. Unplug the washer and remove the lower front access panel.
  2. Place a towel and container underneath the drain filter.
  3. Unscrew the drain pump filter and clean out any debris.
  4. Check the drain hose at the back — ensure it’s not kinked or clogged.
  5. Reconnect, plug back in, and run a short rinse cycle to test.

This solves 90% of drainage issues — no repairman needed.
