Reset Whirlpool Top Loader

If your Whirlpool top-loading washer is stuck or acting up, follow these reset steps:

  1. Unplug the washer from the wall outlet for 1 full minute.
  2. Plug it back in and lift the lid up and down 6 times within 12 seconds.
  3. Each time the lid is opened and closed, it must be done fully for the reset to register.
  4. You should hear a click and the washer should reset.
  5. Try starting a rinse & spin cycle to test if it worked.

This manual reset clears most software glitches on Whirlpool top loaders.
