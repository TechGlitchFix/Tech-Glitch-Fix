Kogan Air Fryer Won’t Turn On

If your Kogan air fryer won’t power up, follow these simple steps:

  1. Check the power cord is securely plugged in and the outlet works.
  2. Open and close the basket firmly — it must click into place.
  3. Ensure the timer or temperature dial is turned past “0”.
  4. If there's still no power, unplug it for 5 minutes and try again.
  5. Test another appliance in the same outlet to confirm it’s not the socket.

This usually resets the fryer and gets it running again.
