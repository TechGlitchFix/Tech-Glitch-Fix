iPhone Not Charging After iOS Update

If your iPhone stopped charging after the latest iOS update, try this:

  1. Force restart: hold the side + volume down button until you see the Apple logo.
  2. Use a different cable and charger to rule out hardware faults.
  3. Go to Settings > Battery > Battery Health & Charging and toggle Optimized Charging off.
  4. Clean the charging port gently using a dry toothbrush or soft brush.
  5. If the issue persists, try resetting all settings under Settings > General > Transfer or Reset > Reset All Settings.

Charging glitches are common after updates — this usually sorts it out fast.
