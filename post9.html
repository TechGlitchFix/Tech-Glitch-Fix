Foxtel Box Stuck on Starting Up

If your Foxtel iQ box is frozen on the startup screen, try this fix:

  1. Turn off the power at the wall and unplug the box completely.
  2. Wait 60 seconds to allow a full reset of the internal components.
  3. Plug it back in and power it on while holding down the Standby button on the box.
  4. Keep holding until the screen changes — this may take up to 30 seconds.
  5. Once the box reboots, check for software updates under Settings.

This soft reboot method usually clears startup glitches without wiping recordings.
