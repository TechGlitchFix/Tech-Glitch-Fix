Hisense TV Remote Not Working

Having trouble with your Hisense remote? Here's a fast fix:

  1. Remove the batteries and press all buttons to discharge static buildup.
  2. Reinsert new batteries — make sure they’re placed correctly (+/-).
  3. Point the remote at your phone’s camera and press a button — if the IR light flashes, the remote is working.
  4. If no flash, the remote is dead. If it flashes but the TV doesn’t respond, power cycle the TV.
  5. Unplug the TV from the wall for 1 minute, plug back in, and retry.

Still no luck? Try a universal remote or the Hisense RemoteNOW app.
