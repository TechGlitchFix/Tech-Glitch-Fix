Fisher & Paykel Compressor Noise

Noticing loud humming or buzzing from your Fisher & Paykel fridge? Here’s how to check and fix it:

  1. Make sure the fridge is level on all sides — use a spirit level if possible.
  2. Ensure the appliance is not touching the wall or cabinets. Leave 5cm space around it.
  3. Unplug and carefully clean the rear compressor fan with a soft brush or air duster.
  4. Listen after plugging it back in — if noise reduces, dust or obstruction was likely the cause.
  5. If noise continues loudly and constantly, contact Fisher & Paykel support. It may be a faulty compressor coil.
